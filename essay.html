On the nature of Jiggle.

Hooke's law of elasticity is an approximation that states that the extension of a spring is in direct proportion with the load applied to it.

Hooke's law is the best law. It has limitless potential in its application to procedural animation. By manipulating a few parameters, a single equation gives rise to an enormous range of personalities.

force     =  -strength * ( value - rest );
velocity  += force / mass;
velocity  *= damping;
value     += velocity;
